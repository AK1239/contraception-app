Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 12, 2026
App version: 1.0.0

Introduction

ContraSafe ("we," "our," or "the app") is an evidence-based contraceptive recommendation tool designed for healthcare providers and the general public. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and your choices.

Summary: ContraSafe works entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal or health data with servers, advertisers, or third-party analytics services.

Information We Do Not Collect

ContraSafe does not require an account and does not ask for:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Location data or device identifiers sent to our servers
  • Payment or financial information

The app does not use analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. There are no network requests made to transmit your health data.

Information Stored on Your Device

To provide personalized contraceptive guidance, the app may store the following locally on your phone or tablet using secure on-device storage:

  • Health and reproductive information you enter in questionnaires — such as age, menstrual cycle details, pregnancy and breastfeeding history, medical conditions, medications, and related clinical factors used to assess contraceptive eligibility
  • Personalization preferences — such as future pregnancy intent, method preferences, and lifestyle factors
  • App settings — your selected language (English or Kiswahili), user role (healthcare provider or general public), and onboarding status
  • Session-only calculator data — some tools (such as natural method calculators, emergency contraception safety checks, and sterilization eligibility screens) keep answers in memory only while you use them and are cleared when the app is closed

This data is used solely to generate recommendations and eligibility results within the app. It never leaves your device through the app itself.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used only to:

  • Calculate contraceptive eligibility based on WHO Medical Eligibility Criteria
  • Personalize method recommendations and comparisons
  • Run natural method and emergency contraception safety calculators
  • Remember your language and role preferences between sessions

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or any purpose outside the app.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your health information with third parties. ContraSafe does not connect to cloud databases, social networks, or third-party data processors.

You may choose to share your screen or device with another person manually; that is outside the app's control and is your responsibility.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you:

  • Use Start Over within a questionnaire to reset that flow
  • Clear the app's storage through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data

Children and Sensitive Information

ContraSafe addresses reproductive health topics and may be used by individuals aged 10 and above as part of eligibility assessments. If you are under 18, we encourage you to use this app with guidance from a parent, guardian, or qualified healthcare provider.

The app may ask about sensitive health topics including HIV status, sexual health, and pregnancy-related history. This information is treated with care and stored only on your device for the purposes described above.

Security

Because all data stays on your device, the security of your information depends largely on how you protect your phone or tablet — such as using a screen lock, keeping your operating system updated, and not sharing your device with untrusted parties.

Third-Party Content

ContraSafe's recommendations are based on publicly available clinical guidelines from the World Health Organization (WHO) and the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). These organizations do not receive your personal data through the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
